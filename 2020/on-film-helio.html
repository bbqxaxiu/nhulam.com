On Film: Helio-

September 20th, 2020

I've moved to Gainesville! (Home is just a 2 hour drive away, and I come back every three weeks or so.) My roommates are a girl I knew in high school, her boyfriend, and another couple. I think living with people who you aren't that close with is really good for you, because you can still feel like you're on your own. Also, I have my own bathroom here, which I've only ever had in London when I was abroad...I feel like I can never go back to sharing a bathroom again.
